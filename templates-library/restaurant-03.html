Our BBQ Legacy

Ember & Oak was born from a passion for authentic, slow-smoked BBQ. We've been perfecting our craft for over 20 years, using traditional smoking methods and premium meats.

Every cut is carefully selected and smoked for 8+ hours over oak and hickory wood, creating the perfect bark and incredibly tender, smoky meat.
