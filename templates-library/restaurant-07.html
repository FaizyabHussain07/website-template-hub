Our Heritage

Founded in 1985, Le Petit Bistro brings authentic Parisian bistro culture to the heart of our city. Our chef trained in Lyon and Paris, mastering the classical techniques of French cuisine.

We honor traditions while celebrating the best of seasonal ingredients. From coq au vin to duck confit, every dish is prepared with precision and passion.
